Recreating
Zealot Hockey into a Standalone Game: VHL - Virtual Hockey League

We are a team of three experienced developers who are passionate about Starcraft 2 Zealot Hockey. As some of the top players in the game, we decided to create a standalone version of it using our own solution

Expertise in Zealot Hockey

As some of the best players in the Starcraft 2 Zealot Hockey game, we're intimately familiar with its mechanics and intricacies. With our in-depth knowledge of the game, we're creating a standalone version that's true to its roots and sure to please fans of the original.

Multiplatform and Accessible

Our game will be available on multiple platforms, making it easy for anyone to access and play. Whether you're on a PC, Mac, Console or Mobile device, you'll be able to enjoy the game without any barriers.

Cutting-Edge Game Design

We're creating a game that's not only fun to play but also accessible and enjoyable for all. With a focus on responsive design and user-friendly interfaces, our game will provide an immersive and enjoyable experience for players of all skill levels.

Features

Here are some of the exciting features that we're working on to make our game stand out.

Fast-Paced Gameplay

Experience the thrill of high-speed gameplay in our top-down hockey game. With fast-paced action and quick reflexes, you'll need to stay on your toes as you compete against other skilled players. Whether you're playing alone or with friends, the intensity never lets up.

Stunning Visuals

Experience jaw-dropping graphics in our top-down hockey game. From the players' intricate movements to the details of the ice, our stunning visuals will immerse you in the game like never before.

Full Support

We are committed to providing full support to our players. We value our community and are always willing to listen to feedback and address any issues that may arise. We understand that bugs can be frustrating, but we promise to work tirelessly to resolve them quickly and efficiently. With our full support, players can enjoy a smooth and enjoyable gaming experience.

Multiplayer Mode

Feature a fully supported multiplayer mode, allowing players to connect and compete with others from around the world. We understand the importance of a seamless multiplayer experience, and we're dedicated to ensuring that our game runs smoothly and reliably for all players.

Offline Training Mode

Allows players to hone their skills and strategies without the need for an internet connection. Whether you're on the go or in a location with limited connectivity, you can access this mode and improve your gameplay at your own pace. We believe in providing our players with the flexibility and convenience to play the game whenever and wherever they want.

Built with Unreal Engine 5

With the latest technology, players will experience a new level of realism and detail in the game world. Additionally, Unreal Engine 5 allows for easy development and implementation of new features, ensuring that our game will continue to evolve and provide an unparalleled gaming experience.

New Version of Nets

25 September 2022

/

Made by: Poodle

We believe that Zealot Hockey nets are very robust and doesn't fit into the fast paced hockey game as much. We decided to have them closer to board and not a square type. Reasoning for that are faster plays from behind the net, better dunks and smaller crease will be all in the current version. We want to keep Ranked games, Unranked games and Custom games fast paced. League might differ in mode just by size of the rink to the width to increase amount of angles and more space for passes and outplaying enemy.
